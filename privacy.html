Privacy Policy — Golf

Last updated: 28 June 2026

This policy describes what the Golf app collects and how it is used.

What we collect

  • Email address — only if you choose to sign in to use the leaderboard, save scores, or join tournaments. Sign-in uses a one-time code sent to your email.
  • Game data — your display name and round scores, stored so they can appear on leaderboards.

You can play the game without signing in; in that case no personal information is collected.

How it is stored

Account and score data is stored using Supabase, our backend provider. Data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections.

What we do not do

  • We do not sell your data.
  • We do not use third-party advertising or tracking.
  • We do not collect your location.

Deleting your data

To delete your account and associated scores, contact adamohanian@gmail.com.
